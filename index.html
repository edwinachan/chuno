Chuno

Rules of the game

If you've played Uno before, you'll know how to play Chuno.

  1. Each player starts with two cards in their hand
  2. The card on top of the deck will be removed and will form the start of the discard pile
  3. The first player is chosen randomly
  4. A player can discard a card from their hand if either the colour or number matches that of the discard pile. This card should then be placed face up on the discard pile
  5. Each player can only discard one card per turn
  6. The first player to have an empty hand wins
